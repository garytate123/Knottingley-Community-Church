Welcome to KCC...

Knottingley Community Church is a small church with a big heart. We are part of the Elim family of churches, which accounts for approximately 550 churches in the UK. Elim is a denomination. What is a denomination? A denomination is a category of Church and the Elim Pentecostal denomination was founded in 1915 and gained great impetus through an outstanding young evangelist and church planter called George Jeffreys. You can read ore about Elim Pentecostal denomination here.

There is no better way to find out what KCC is like then to visit us on a Sunday morning. If you are new to church, there is nothing to worry about. We welcome you to come as you are, there is no need to dress in your Sunday best, unless that's what you feel conformable in. There will be someone to welcome you at the door and you can always let us know it's your first time and we'll give you all the you need.

The service starts at 10:30am(don't worry if you are late) and finishes around 12pm. Coffee and tea are served afterwards for those that would like to chat afterwards.

During the service, there will be notices, bible readings, prayer, worship, bible teaching and communion. Communion is a time when we are reminded of God's love for us through the sacrifice of His Son Jesus.
